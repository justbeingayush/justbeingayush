# 🌌 Beyond the Screen

So, what happens when I actually step away from the keyboard? A lot, actually! I think having hobbies offline is the best way to stay creative online. 

## 🪙 The Coin Vault (Numismatics)
I'm a massive coin nerd. There's just something incredibly cool about the history and art stamped into physical currency. I specifically hunt down rare, limited-edition, or discontinued Indian and foreign coins.

## 📸 Catching the Vibe
I love framing the world through a lens. I shoot mostly with my Cason CS6 4K action camera, which makes it super fun to experiment with lighting and weird angles on the go. (Keep an eye out for that *@ayu.vibee* tag!)

## 🎧 Music 24/7
I literally cannot work in silence. Whether I'm debugging a Python script or studying, there is always music playing. Harry Styles, Benson Boone, Chainsmokers etc. just name them and you will probably find it on my most-played list. It just sets the perfect vibe to get things done.
